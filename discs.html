What type of disc should I choose?

There are many, many types of folfing discs out there, and each has a time and place. Discs are typically put into one of three categories: driver, mid-range, and putter. There are subcategories of these, and a lot of variation even within those, but if you are new to Disc Golf it shouldn't matter much what you use at first.

Drivers are for long distance throws, and are a good choice for your first disc, if you're in that market.
Putters are very stable and easy to throw straight. They are nigh impossible to throw very far, however.
Mid-range discs are usually the last to be added to a folfer's collection. They combine the predictable flight path and ease-of-use of a putter with the distance and curving capabilities of drivers.
