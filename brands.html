Disc Manufacturers:

  • Innova
    Innova is the most popular brand of folfing disc in the Spokane area. Their discs are available at every retailer. Most of their discs come with statistics printed right on them, which is convenient for choosing a new disc or comparing old ones.
  • Discraft
    Discraft is another hugely popular manufacturer of Disc Golf equipment. Their rating system seems simple at first, but can be a little more tricky to figure out than Innova's. They also make gear for other disc-based games and sports.
