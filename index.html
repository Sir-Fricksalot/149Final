Welcome to my Disc Golf Site!

This website is intended to provide encouragement, information, and inspiration to new and prospective disc golfers in the Spokane, Washington area. Please feel free to peruse the pages of this website for any relevant topics or information you might need. If you are brand new to disc golfing, you might want to visit the glossary first to familiarize yourself with some basic folfing terminology. Some of it is unofficial, but you'll still find it useful when communicating with other disk golfers out on the course.
