  • How much does it cost to play Disc Golf?
    Most courses in the area are free, although some do charge a few bucks for parking. Discs range from $10-$20.

  • How can I improve quickly?
    Play with more people! The best way to improve your game is by watching and getting tips from more experienced players. Don't be afraid to ask to join another group and get some pointers!

  • How long does it take to play a round of Disc Golf?
    An average round of 18 holes takes about an hour and a half. It can easily take longer if you have a large group or if the course is crowded.
